STRUCT Infra Consultants Pvt. Ltd.

STRUCT Infra Consultants Pvt. Ltd. is a civil and structural engineering firm that offers consultancy services for Infrastructure & other Engineering areas. Established in October 2020, iSTRUCT is an employee-owned company that provides engineering services for infrastructure projects and specializes in all types of normal bridges.

STRUCT was started by a group of four colleagues with a strong passion for Structural & Highway engineering. We work closely with our clients and partners and take on their challenges as if they were our own. Our clients choose us for our commitment to safety and quality service, because our relationships are rooted in trust, and our capable team have the excitement and skill to tackle challenging problems.

Sectors

Bridges and Flyovers

Being our key business focus, we provide detailed engineering services for a range of Infrastructure projects such as Highways and expressways, Urban flyovers, Rail Over Bridges, Major river bridges etc. With practical experience in both design and erection engineering of bridges, we can develop concepts around practical means of construction from the start to deliver fully integrated solutions tailored to each project. Our specialties include:

  • Long-span cable-stayed and suspension bridges
  • Short- and medium-span concrete and steel bridges
  • Bridge launching and construction engineering
  • Bridge deconstruction design
  • Bridge rehabilitation and load evaluation services
